Sleep Monitor Setup

This web-page will guide you through the setup for the Sleep Monitor.

Step 1: Plug in your Sleep Monitor to your Router

  1. Use the provided ethernet cable to connect the device to the router. While you are there, note down the password for your Wifi network. On some wireless routers, it is stuck on the router with a sticker. The WiFi password is also sometimes called a "WPA2 Key".
  2. Use the provided power adapter to plug in the device to the wall
Once this is done, please press to go step 2.

Step 2: Searching for the Sleep Monitor
